I am a postdoctoral researcher at University of California, Davis working with Prof. Prem Devanbu. I did my PhD from the University of Texas at Austin under the supervision of Prof. Miryung Kim.

I am primarily interested in Software Engineering research with a focus on empirical studies, program analysis, and software evolution. Using techniques from diverse domains including machine learning, natural language processing, and rigorous statistical modeling, I analyze large scale software repositories to understand on-going software engineering practices. This data-driven knowledge helps me build novel program analysis techniques and development tools to improve software quality and programmer productivity.
